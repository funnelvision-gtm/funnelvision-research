# FunnelVision - Customer & Partner Research

## Company Overview

**FunnelVision** (https://funnelvision.cloud) helps GTM teams maintain HubSpot deal data hygiene. Sales leaders set up rules to identify missing or outdated data, which triggers automated update requests delivered to sales reps via Slack or Microsoft Teams. The approach focuses on human behaviour — meeting reps where they already are — rather than pure automation.

**Target Market:** RevOps teams, starting UK-first then expanding to Europe, Australia, Canada, and the US.
