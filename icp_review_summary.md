# FunnelVision — ICP Review Summary

## What This Document Is

A single reference that connects FunnelVision's ideal customer and partner profiles to the real pains expressed by 228 competitor reviewers across Scratchpad (133), Supered (38), Momentum (32), and Rattle (25). Every claim is backed by named quotes from real buyers in the market.

---

## The Market Opportunity in One Paragraph

Across 228 reviews, the same story repeats: CRM data is dirty, reps don't fill things in, managers waste hours chasing updates, forecasts suffer, and deals fall through the cracks. Scratchpad, Rattle, and Momentum all solve pieces of this — but only for Salesforce. Supered solves it for HubSpot but from inside the CRM. **Nobody delivers rules-based HubSpot data hygiene enforcement through Slack/Teams.** That's FunnelVision's space.
