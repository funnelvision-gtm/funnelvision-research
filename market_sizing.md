## TAM (Total Addressable Market)

**"Every HubSpot paying customer that uses deals/pipeline"**

Not all 228,706 HubSpot customers use Sales Hub with deals pipeline. HubSpot doesn't disclose tier breakdowns, but we can estimate:

- HubSpot reports average revenue per customer of $11,683/yr
- Sales Hub Pro starts at ~$450/mo ($5,400/yr) for 5 users
- Many customers are on Starter ($20/mo) or Free tiers
- 62% of Pro+ customers use multiple hubs, suggesting Sales Hub is commonly bundled

**Conservative estimate:** ~40-50% of paying customers use Sales Hub with deals pipeline = **90,000-115,000 companies**

**At £5,000/year per customer:**

| Scenario | Customers | TAM |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| Conservative (90K) | 90,000 | **£450M / ~$570M** |
| Mid-range (100K) | 100,000 | **£500M / ~$630M** |
| Optimistic (115K) | 115,000 | **£575M / ~$725M** |

## SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)

**"HubSpot customers who have a sales team large enough to have data hygiene problems, in regions FunnelVision can serve"**

Filters applied:
1. **Has a real sales team (5+ reps)** — solo users and very small teams don't have data hygiene problems at scale. This likely eliminates 50-60% of Sales Hub users.
2. **English-speaking or English-first markets** — UK, Europe, Australia, Canada, US. This covers ~90% of HubSpot's customer base.
3. **Pro or Enterprise tier** — companies on Starter/Free likely don't have the maturity or budget for a data hygiene tool.

**Estimate:** ~20-30% of TAM = **18,000-35,000 companies**

| Scenario | Customers | SAM |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| Conservative (18K) | 18,000 | **£90M / ~$113M** |
| Mid-range (25K) | 25,000 | **£125M / ~$158M** |
| Optimistic (35K) | 35,000 | **£175M / ~$221M** |

## SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)

**"What FunnelVision can realistically capture in the next 3-5 years"**

Factors:
- Early-stage company with partner-led GTM
- 6 partners signed, covering UK + parts of Europe + Australia + US
- HubSpot ecosystem presence still being built
- Category is nascent — buyer education required

**Year 1-2 target:** Capture customers through existing partner network + direct sales

## The Broader Market Context

### CRM Market
- Global CRM market: **$112.9 billion** (2025), growing to $321 billion by 2034 (12.4% CAGR)
- HubSpot's share: ~5.6% of CRM market

### Revenue Operations Software Market
- **$3.7 billion** (2023), projected to reach **$15.9 billion** by 2033 (15.4% CAGR)
- RevOps market overall: $6.16 billion (2025), projected $21.7 billion by 2032 (17.2% CAGR)

### The Data Quality Problem
- **76%** of CRM users say less than half their data is accurate and complete
- **37%** report losing revenue directly due to poor data quality
- Poor data quality costs organisations an average of **$12.9M annually**
- Only **11%** of RevOps teams rate their data quality as "excellent"

### Why This Matters for FunnelVision's Story
At £5,000/year, FunnelVision is priced at **less than 0.04%** of the average annual cost of poor data quality ($12.9M). The ROI case is almost absurdly easy to make — even preventing one lost deal due to bad data pays for the tool many times over.

## Market Sizing Assumptions & Caveats

1. **TAM is HubSpot-only** — if FunnelVision ever expands to Salesforce, the TAM multiplies ~5-10x (Salesforce has 150,000+ customers)
2. **Flat pricing simplifies the model** — per-user pricing would change the TAM calculation significantly
3. **Partner channel is a multiplier** — Siloy/ProsperOHub alone has 650+ implementations, many of which could be FunnelVision targets
4. **HubSpot's customer base is growing 19% YoY** — the TAM is expanding automatically
5. **No competitor owns this exact space in HubSpot** — first-mover advantage is real but temporary
