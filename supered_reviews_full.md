## Key Observation for FunnelVision

**Supered's reviewer base is overwhelmingly HubSpot partners/consultants** — not end-users. This is significant:
- 90%+ of Supered reviews come from agency owners, consultants, and HubSpot partners
- Only 2 of 38 reviewers are clearly from mid-market end-user companies (Kevin S. and Diana Marcela G.)
- This suggests Supered's go-to-market is heavily partner-driven (similar to FunnelVision)
- The partner reviewers describe deploying Supered *for their clients* — they're the buyers, not the end-users
- FunnelVision's partner personas (Rachel, Justin) would recognise many of these reviewers as peers
